About The Karrsh Captures

We frame your moments with passion, creativity, and precision. Our mission is to turn your special moments into timeless works of art.

Who We Are

At The Karrsh Captures (TKC), we believe that every moment is a story waiting to be told. Our freelance photography agency, headquartered in the bustling city of Delhi, India, specializes in capturing memories that stand the test of time.

Whether it’s the joyous laughter at a wedding or the sophistication of a corporate gala, we are driven by the desire to craft visually compelling narratives. With years of experience and an unwavering passion for excellence, we ensure each frame reflects the emotion and beauty of the occasion.
